Games

A small browser-game shelf for arcade experiments, action prototypes, and older projects worth keeping playable.

Rift Runner

A fast survival shooter set inside a collapsing star rift, built around escalating enemy waves, upgrade choices, and score chasing.

Starfall Survivor

An early bullet-heaven style prototype where your weapons fire on their own and the real game is movement, leveling, and building toward screen-filling chaos.

Gloam Delve

A compact roguelike crawl with turn-based movement, bite-sized dungeon floors, scavenged upgrades, and just enough danger to make every step matter.

Sunforge Idle

A clicker and idle baseline about feeding a volatile core, scaling into automation, and overclocking the whole machine for permanent long-run bonuses.

Shardhand

A combat-first deckbuilder prototype with energy, card rewards, enemy intents, and enough deck shaping to start experimenting with build identity.

Lantern Road

A text-based RPG adventure prototype built around stat checks, resource choices, branching routes, and multiple story endings.

Cosmic Journey

A classic arcade-style space shooter with multiple stages, varied enemy sets, and an older-school action feel.
