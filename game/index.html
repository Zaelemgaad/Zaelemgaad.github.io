Astral Tempest

Launch Sequence

Move with WASD or Arrow Keys. On touch screens, hold and drag anywhere on the arena to steer. Your ship auto-fires at nearby targets, and Space or the dash button cuts through danger when the burst is charged.

Mission

Survive as long as you can. Destroy enemies to earn score, pull in pulse shards to power your run, and ride each new wave bonus to keep the arena from overwhelming you.

Touch note: press and drag in the arena to move, then tap Dash Burst when the screen gets crowded.
